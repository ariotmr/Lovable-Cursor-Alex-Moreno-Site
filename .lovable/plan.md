

# Alex Moreno — Personal Trainer Booking Website

## Overview
A single-page, mobile-first website for a Barcelona-based strength & conditioning coach. Dark/neutral aesthetic with orange accents. Every section drives toward booking. Demo booking buttons (toast confirmation) for now.

---

## Sections

### 1. Hero
- Dark background with subtle fitness Unsplash image overlay
- Bold headline: "Structured Strength Training in Barcelona"
- Subline mentioning indoor studio + outdoor sessions
- Orange "Book Your Session" button (scrolls to schedule + shows toast)
- "View Schedule" text link (smooth scroll)
- Full-viewport height, centered content

### 2. Schedule — Full Week Grid
- Mon–Sat timetable grid layout
- Morning / midday / evening time slots
- Each cell shows: class name, focus tag (Strength/Conditioning/Mobility), location badge (Studio 🏠 / Outdoor 🌳), duration, spots available
- Orange "Book" button per class
- Clicking "Book" shows a toast: "Demo — booking confirmed for [class]"
- Responsive: horizontal scroll or stacked cards on mobile

### 3. Trust — Coach Profile
- Coach portrait (Unsplash stock)
- Name, title, short bio (2 lines max)
- 3 credibility bullets (e.g. "10+ years coaching experience", "Structured periodized programming", "500+ clients trained")
- Two side-by-side images: clean studio interior + Barcelona outdoor training

### 4. Social Proof
- 3–4 short testimonials (1–2 lines each)
- Each with first name, age, profession
- Card layout with subtle styling
- One community training photo strip (studio + outdoor mix)

### 5. Logistics
- Clean two-column grid with icon-led items:
  - 📍 Base: Barcelona (Eixample studio)
  - 🌳 Outdoor: Ciutadella Park, Barceloneta Beach, Montjuïc
  - ⏱ Sessions: 55 min
  - 🎒 What to bring
  - ✅ Who it's for / ❌ Who it's not for

### 6. Final CTA
- Large dark section with orange CTA button
- Short direct copy: "Ready to train? Book your next session."
- Same demo toast behavior

---

## Design System
- **Base colors**: Dark charcoal background (#1a1a1a), off-white text
- **Accent**: Orange (~hsl(25, 95%, 53%)) for CTAs, badges, highlights only
- **Typography**: Strong hierarchy — large bold headings, clean body text
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, grid-aligned
- **Images**: Unsplash stock photos for all imagery

## Navigation
- Sticky top nav: Logo/name + section links + "Book Now" orange button
- Smooth scroll to sections
